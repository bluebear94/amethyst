Why does, for example, calling $:cdr on a large list take so much time?

Amethyst vigilantly clones values on assignment, in order to avoid unnecessary mutation and model TI-Basic. Chances are that you can use a function such as $:foldl to do the same thing. Alternatively, you can do $:valcopy(0) to disable this behavior, if you're writing in a functional style or know what you're doing.

Why did you change the syntax for equality?

It could be a source of potential confusion.
